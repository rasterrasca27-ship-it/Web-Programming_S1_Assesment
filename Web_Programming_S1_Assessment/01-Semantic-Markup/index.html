chocolate Chip Cookies

Delicious homemade cookies with gooey chocolate chips.

Prep: 15 mins Bake: 10 mins Yields: 24 cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
Baker's Note: No eggs? No problem. Swap each egg with 1/4 cup of applesauce, mashed bananas, or yogurt for a perfectly soft texture.

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 190°C.
  2. Mix dry ingredients: In a small bowl, whisk the flour and baking soda together. Set aside.
  3. Cream butter and sugars: In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract until beautifully smooth and fluffy.
  4. Add eggs: Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  5. Combine: Gradually add the flour mixture to the wet ingredients, folding gently until just combined.
  6. The best part: Stir in the chocolate chips until evenly distributed.
  7. Scoop: Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto ungreased baking sheets, leaving space for them to spread.
  8. Bake: Bake in the oven for 8-10 minutes. The edges should be golden brown while the centers remain soft.
  9. Cool: Allow the cookies to rest on the baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring them to wire racks to cool completely.
